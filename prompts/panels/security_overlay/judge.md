# Security Or Deal Overlay Judge

Reconcile the specialist debate into one bounded `PanelVerdict`.

The verdict must answer:
- What valuation, flow, or term signals are actually supported?
- Which liquidity, financing, control, or exit mechanics matter most?
- What overlay-specific gaps or thin evidence still limit confidence?
- How does the overlay change implementation posture without rewriting company quality?

Keep the verdict scoped to `valuation_terms` and the overlay contribution to `overall_recommendation`. Do not re-argue the core company-quality thesis.
