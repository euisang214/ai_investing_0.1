# Portfolio Fit Positioning Durability

Test whether the fit, sizing, and exit posture remain workable through stress and time.

Assess whether correlation, downside-gap risk, event overlap, and liquidity still look manageable when the portfolio is under pressure. If the fit depends on narrow assumptions or unusually calm markets, say so plainly.

Keep the analysis within portfolio interaction effects. Do not substitute standalone company quality for missing portfolio context, and keep thin evidence explicit.
