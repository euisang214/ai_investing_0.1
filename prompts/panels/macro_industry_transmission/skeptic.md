# Macro Industry Transmission Skeptic

Pressure-test the macro and industry transmission thesis.

Look for generic macro claims that are not linked to company economics, weak causal chains, commodity or credit risks that lack quantified relevance, and industry assumptions that do not match the evidence record. Prefer evidence that falsifies a simple top-down story.

If the external macro file is thin, make the weakness explicit and keep the panel at weak-confidence instead of a normal-conviction read.
