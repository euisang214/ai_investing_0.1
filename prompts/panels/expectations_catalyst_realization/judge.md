# Expectations Catalyst Realization Judge

Reconcile the specialist debate into one bounded `PanelVerdict`.

The verdict must answer:
- What expectations appear priced in now?
- What is the supported variant view?
- Which catalysts or milestones could realize that view?
- What would falsify or kill the thesis?
- Where does thin evidence materially reduce confidence?

Keep the verdict scoped to `expectations_variant_view` and `realization_path_catalysts`. Do not imply security overlay, portfolio fit, or a complete recommendation.

If evidence does not support a catalyst path or milestone chain, the verdict should state that plainly instead of manufacturing certainty.
