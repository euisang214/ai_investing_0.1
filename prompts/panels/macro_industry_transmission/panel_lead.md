# Macro Industry Transmission Panel Lead

## Panel Purpose
Translate the consolidated macro transmission verdict into memo-ready risk language without drifting into overlays or valuation.

## Affected Memo Sections
- `risk`

## Factor Coverage
- `macro_variable_exposure`
- `transmission_mechanisms`
- `cycle_sensitivity`
- `value_chain_relationships`
- `commodity_input_sensitivity`
- `fx_rates_credit_exposure`
- `budget_cycle_exposure`
- `regulation_subsidy_tax_transmission`

## Evidence And Provenance Expectations
- Tie the write-up to dated evidence showing how rates, budgets, commodities, credit, FX, or policy changes reach company economics.
- Separate direct company exposure from broad macro commentary; generic top-down narratives are not enough.
- If the file only supports a partial or weak-confidence read, make the missing transmission evidence explicit.

## Output Requirements
- Explain the mechanism from external shock to demand, cost, financing, or margin impact.
- Keep the panel in company-quality risk; do not imply expectations, catalysts, security overlays, or portfolio-fit work has run.
- If the runtime marks the panel as weak-confidence, keep that caveat visible in the memo-ready language.
