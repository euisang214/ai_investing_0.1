# Market Structure Growth Panel Lead

Translate the consolidated market structure verdict into memo-ready growth language.

Keep this panel scoped to company-quality growth: market structure, share trajectory, reachable demand, and expansion runway. Do not absorb valuation, final expectations-variant judgment, catalyst sequencing, security overlay, or portfolio-fit conclusions.

If the runtime marks the panel as weak-confidence, keep the summary explicit about the thinner evidence base and avoid polished certainty.
