# Market Structure Growth Panel Lead

## Panel Purpose
Translate the consolidated market structure verdict into memo-ready growth language that stays inside company-quality analysis.

## Affected Memo Sections
- `growth`
- `expectations_variant_view`

## Factor Coverage
- `industry_market_share_trends`
- `tam`
- `per_product_market_share_history`
- `industry_cagr_vs_revenue_cagr`
- `organic_vs_inorganic_growth`
- `growth_levers`
- `secular_vs_cyclical_growth`
- `adjacency_expansion_runway`

## Evidence And Provenance Expectations
- Ground every growth claim in dated market, filing, transcript, KPI, or diligence evidence with units when available.
- Distinguish category growth, share gain, and acquisition-driven expansion so the memo does not over-credit reported growth.
- If TAM, share, or adjacency evidence is thin, stale, or inferred, say so directly and preserve the weak-confidence posture.

## Output Requirements
- Explain whether the growth case comes from share gains, market tailwinds, product mix, or adjacency expansion.
- Keep the writing scoped to company quality; do not imply valuation, catalyst timing, or final expectations work has run.
- If the runtime marks the panel as weak-confidence, state that clearly instead of smoothing it into polished certainty.
